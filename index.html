Course Details

EASA Modules Exams Preparation

This course is designed to prepare students for the EASA Modules Exams, essential for becoming a licensed engineer. It covers all necessary topics and provides comprehensive study materials and practice tests to ensure success.

Applying for EASA License

This course guides students through the process of applying for an EASA license from both the Hungary and Greece authorities. It covers the required documentation, procedures, and ensures compliance with regulatory standards.

Preparing SOJT for A320 and B737NG

Students enrolled in this course will receive specialized training to prepare for the practical On-the-Job Training (SOJT) required for working with Airbus A320 and Boeing B737NG aircraft. The course focuses on hands-on experience and real-world scenarios.

Type Courses for Aircraft Series

This course offers in-depth training for specific aircraft series, including the Airbus A320, Boeing B787, Airbus A330, and Boeing B737. Students will gain comprehensive knowledge of aircraft systems, maintenance procedures, and regulatory requirements.

Zoom Online and Physical In-Class

This course provides flexible learning options, allowing students to attend classes either through Zoom online sessions or in-person at our physical classroom facilities. It accommodates diverse learning preferences and schedules.
